Odd Future Wolf Gang Kill Them All

Odd Future Wolf Gang Kill Them All, more commonly know as Odd Future or simply OF, is one of the most influential rap groups of the last decade. The group, founded in 2007, consisted of over ten members of different rappers, artists, and producers. They rose quickly to popularity with their unique sound of alternative hip-hop. Agressive, out-their, and seriously not giving a f**k are, all words that come to mind when reminiscing on the group. Their concerts were known for extreme hype, mosh-pits, and a lot of adrenaline. The group also had a huge influnece on street-wear and even released their own Odd Future line which still has a place in fashion today. The group split up a few years ago so that members could pursue their own (very sucessful) solo careers but all hip-hop fans will forever know the importance of Odd Future in the music world.

Tyler. the Creator

The leader and one of the main founders of Odd Future, Tyler. the Creator, was a key part of the OF lifestyle and sound. He was also the star of the Odd Future comedy show Loiter Squad which aired on Comedy Central for a few years. Since his Odd Future days, Tyler has released five studio albums, with his most previous one winning an Oscar. He is one of the most iconic hip-hop artist in the game today.
