An Album a Day Keeps the Corona Virus Away

Being stuck in quarantine sucks. You know what doesnâ€™t? Good music. Throughout the quarantine I have listened to a new album (almost) everyday to help me keep my head in a good space. It has been an awesome journey so far and I have discovered some incredible new bands and artists. Below are album recommendation/reviews for whatever quarantine mood you may be in! Want something upbeat and energetic to blast through your speakers and scare away the Corona Virus? Definitely go check out How To Be A Human Being, Eternal Atake, or GO:OD AM. Need something a bit more mellow while having amazing music in the background? The Clarence Greenwood Recordings, Love Slacker, ZABA, or So Long Forever are perfect options. Perhaps some new-age experimental rap and hip hop will cheer you up: After Hours, Ego Death, Bad Sports, or CARE FOR ME are your solution. Of course I have some great Alternative//Indie Rock selection if that is more your sound: The New Abnormal, When We Were Friends, Just Cosmo, Goth Babe. Whatever your taste may be, check out some of these quarantine albums - hopefully you can find one that makes you forget about the madness around us.
