Album Length: 9 tracks // 37 minutes

Genre: Alternative Rock // Indie Rock

Best Track: Kilby Girl

Personnel Favorite: Watch Your Mouth

Quick Thoughts: Really loved The Backseat Lovers' sound. They truly encaptured a proper Indie Rock impression with this project. The overall tone of this album was pleasantly cohesive - it was definitely their own music but I also really enjoyed how each song felt a bit different. They definitely captured a lot of emotions throughout - especially about girls - which was not only enjoyable but relateable. They are still a pretty raw sound and have plenty of room to grow.

Would Recommend? If you like any type of alternative music, yes.

Day 12

Album Length: 17 tracks // 1 hour 10 minutes

Genre: Rap // Hip Hop

Best Track: Perfect Circle / God Speed

Personnel Favorite: Break the Law

Quick Thoughts: Man, if you think you favorite rapper has a lot of content, Mac always just blows it out of the water. Citizen Cope

Day 15

Album Length: 13 tracks // 52 minutes

Genre: Indie // Baroque Pop

Best Track: For Her

Personnel Favorite: Cosmonauts

Quick Thoughts: This may be the most unique album I have listened to in quite some time. I will be honest, I only gave it a listen after Pitchfork gave this an astonishing 10/10 (the only 10 of the decade). It did not disappoint. Each track is composed beautifully with sounds wildly different from one an other but somehow connect quite perfectly. It is not necessarily my forte of music however it is something an music-lover can truly appreciate. You can see the passion that Fiona put in to creating this.

Would Recommend? If you are open to explore a different side of music
